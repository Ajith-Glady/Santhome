Who Are We?

Santhome Wayanad Charitable Trust was established on December 19, 2024, to commemorate the 50th anniversary of priesthood of Rev. Fr. Thomas Therakam. Rooted in decades of dedicated service in social work, education, media, and justice for the underprivileged, the trust embodies a legacy of compassion and commitment to uplifting the marginalized.
